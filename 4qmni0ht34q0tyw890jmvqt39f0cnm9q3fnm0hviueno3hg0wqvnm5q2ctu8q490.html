Daspa is a spa for anyone in need of a fresh massage, or a sweet bedtime story. Daspa is a new spa that will take all of your worries away! Press on the "Bundles" tab or "Prices" tab to see what we offer.

Bundles

Look below for what we offer with our Basic and Premium bundles.

Basic Package

With our Basic Package, you will recieve a ten-minute massage from our expert masseur, and a hotel room. Please pick up your room keys at the front desk. You also have a phone for certain needs you may want. Check the note by the phone for your options.

Premium Package

With our all-new Premium Package, you will recieve a luxorious fifteen-minute massage from our expert masseur, hugs, and a hotel room complete with a bedtime story of your choice. Please pick up your jeweled room keys at the front desk. You also have a phone for ANY needs you may want.
